Executive Coaching

I help my clients access the wisdom, clarity, and insight needed to unlock their true potential as leaders, and as human beings.

Who I work with

My clients are accomplished individuals navigating demanding careers while seeking a more grounded, meaningful life. I help them cut through the noise and distraction to focus on what truly matters.

Sometimes they're in the midst of a transition—launching a new venture, stepping into a new role—and sometimes they simply sense they could be doing more with their gifts.

  • Senior executives and VPs navigating leadership transitions
  • Founders and entrepreneurs scaling through rapid growth
  • Technical leaders stepping into broader organizational roles
  • Accomplished individuals—whether high-profile or undiscovered—seeking a rare space for honest reflection

What I bring

As a longtime tech executive at some of the world's most successful companies—and a parent raising two daughters—I've lived these tensions firsthand. I've led teams at Apple, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and Google. I know what it takes to perform at the highest levels, and I know what it costs.

As a recognized Zen meditation teacher, I bring a perspective that reaches well beyond conventional leadership advice. I help my clients access the wisdom, clarity, and insight needed to unlock their true potential as leaders, and as human beings.

And as an ordained Buddhist priest, our conversations are legally privileged and fully confidential—creating a rare space for honesty and candor that high-profile individuals often struggle to find elsewhere.

This combination of experience and training allows me to offer far greater depth than a typical executive coach.

What we work on

Presence and clarity

How do you stay grounded when everything around you moves fast? I help leaders develop the capacity to be fully present—in high-stakes meetings, difficult conversations, and the daily demands of executive life.

Purpose and meaning

Success without meaning is exhausting. I help clients reconnect with what truly matters to them and align their work with their deeper values.

Transitions and growth

New roles, new companies, new chapters. I help leaders navigate change with confidence, letting go of what no longer serves them and stepping into what's next.

Sustainable leadership

You can't lead well if you're burned out. I help leaders find ways to perform at the highest levels without sacrificing their health, relationships, or sense of self.

How it works

I typically recommend meeting twice a month, with email or text support in between if needed. Some clients prefer weekly meetings; others want a monthly check-in. I can support either.

Sessions are held via video or in person if you're in the Bay Area.

The initial commitment is six months. Real change tends to emerge over time, not in isolated conversations. Six months allows patterns to surface and shift in a meaningful way. Most executives continue beyond that.

Investment

The monthly rate is $4,000 for two meetings per month, and scales up or down according to meeting frequency.

This investment reflects the depth and confidentiality of the work, and the unique combination of executive experience and contemplative training I bring to our conversations.

Let's talk

The first step is a conversation. No commitment, no pressure—just a chance to see if we'd work well together.
